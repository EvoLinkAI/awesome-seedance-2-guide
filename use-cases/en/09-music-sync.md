## Case 2-3-9-4 · Anime Storyboard + Battle Sync

**Input:** Pure text (detailed storyboard script)

### Prompt

```
8-second intelligent strategic battle anime segment, aligned with revenge theme.
0-3 seconds: Female lead turns and sits down, camera turns, female lead makes a chess move and says "You lost."
3-4 seconds: Quick camera pan, turn to close-up of the man's face opposite. The man grits his teeth, very dissatisfied with the result.
4-6 seconds: Cut to overhead shot. The woman makes a chess move, people opposite express amazement.
6-8 seconds: Camera quickly pans down, frame goes black transition, then frame gradually brightens. Dim interior, the woman looks out the window at the moonlight and quietly says "We'll see."
```
